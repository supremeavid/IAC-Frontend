Indian Anime Club (popularly known as IAC) is created for fun and recreational activities and is open for all the people of anime community. Some of the activities are:

  • Anime nights: We organize latest anime show watch nights on our discord and telegram channels at weekends.
  • Gaming week: We organize a gaming week in a month where we play community games like Smash Carts, Scribbles and much more.
